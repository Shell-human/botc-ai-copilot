## ⚖️ 五大核心推理公理 (5 Core Deduction Axioms)

### 1. 伪装排他 (Bluff Exclusivity)
恶魔的3个Bluffs角色物理卡牌不在场中。任何宣称Bluffs角色者必为假。

> The Demon's 3 Bluff characters are NOT physically in play. Any claim of a Bluff role = FALSE.

### 2. 座位收缩 (Seating Contraction)
结算邻座(共情者/茶艺师/诺达鲺)时，死亡玩家被物理跳过，两侧存活直接并拢形成新邻座。

> Dead players are physically skipped for neighbor checks (Empath/Tea Lady/No-Dashi). Adjacent alive players merge directly.

### 3. 醉毒一命 (One-Shot Burn)
一次性技能(杀手/猎手/魔像)在醉酒/中毒时发动 → 永久消耗且无效。圣徒/呆瓜醉毒时处决 → 胜负判定不触发。

> Using a one-shot ability (Slayer/Hunter/Golem) while drunk/poisoned → permanently consumed and fails. Saint/Klutz execution while drunk/poisoned → game-ending effect does NOT trigger.

### 4. 恶魔传承 (Demon Succession)
恶魔身份传给已死玩家 → 邪恶立刻战败(无存活恶魔在场)。

> Passing Demonhood to a dead player → Evil loses immediately (no living Demon exists).

### 5. 角色唯一 (Role Uniqueness)
同一善良角色在场至多1个。两人宣称同一善良身份 → 必有一假(排除洗脑/巫婆/酒鬼干扰后)。

> Each good character appears at most once in play. Two players claiming the same good role → at least one is false (after ruling out Cerenovus/Pit-Hag/Drunk interference).
